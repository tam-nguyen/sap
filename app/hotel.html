Ritz - Carlton Berlin

1234 StreetName SW

Your home-from-home during the ERT Plenary Session will be the Ritz-Carlton Berlin, a five-star hotel in the historical heart of Mitte. Located on Potsdamer Platz, this iconic site was once an intersection divided by East and West Germany and this elegant property is testament to the city’s ongoing transformation following the fall of the Berlin Wall.

Close-by, you will find an array of boutiques, restaurants and historic attractions such as Checkpoint Charlie, the Brandenburg Gate and the Berlin Wall Memorial. With its inspiring location and proximity to key attractions, the Ritz-Carlton Berlin provides a captivating backdrop to meet, dine, and socialise whilst exploring the capital city.

One of the charming qualities of the Ritz-Carlton is its welcoming, residential feel across its collection of luxurious guestrooms, penthouse apartments and suites. Another draw is how the hotel encapsulates both the old and the new side of Berlin. One of the Ritz-Carlton’s most recently renovated properties, it boasts many references to the present-day, whilst celebrating the city’s illustrious Golden Era via a modern interpretation of Art Deco design. Look out for the geometric motifs, hand-selected furniture and artwork inspired by movie stars. You’re guaranteed to feel the spirit of Berlin’s most convivial era while you are here.

Aside from the hotel’s rich and vibrant history, the Ritz-Carlton Berlin is an excellent destination for mixology and cuisine. The Curtain Club is a British Gentleman’s-inspired bar and restaurant offering a chic space to enjoy expertly crafted cocktails and delicious cuisine. Meanwhile, the Fragrances Bar is an opulent space where guests can ignite their senses whilst sipping on experimental beverages inspired by aromatic perfumes. By day, The Lounge is a must-visit for guests who would like to sip on the finest blended teas prepared by an expert Sommelier.

With its personalised service, state-of-the-art amenities and air of casual luxury, we’re confident you are going to enjoy your stay here.
